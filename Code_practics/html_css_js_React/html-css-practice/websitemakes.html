COMMITTEE TOR'S

Detailed Situation Analysis

To have a detailed situation analysis, study, research, policy interventions and will give future roadmap & actions to be taken for adoption of Web 3.0 in Punjab

Implementation R&D Regime

To suggest to implementation R&D regime in the context.

Recommendations and Specific actions

To give its recommendations and will propose specific actions to be taken by the government in this regard.

Propose Action Items

To propose action items to launch Metaverse, AR/VR based projects & will suggest related enabling provisions on legal, financial and technical aspects taking into considerations of its applications like virtual currencies, NFTs & other emerging concepts in the domain.

Study All Aspects

To study all aspects of crypto-currencies, virtual currencies, NFTs & Metaverse pertaining to its pros & cons, legal, operational & taxation issues, implications, adoption in reference to trading, mining, legal provisions and all other related issues.

Virtual Office Space

To propose viable options to secure virtual office space for PITB or any other public sector entity in Metaverse, and will propose its legal, transactional and operational model.

Study the Global Best Practices

To Study the global best practices being adopted by different countries on the subject including but not limited to UK, USA, India, China etc.

Engage with all Major Global Virtual Currency Stakeholders

To engage with all major global virtual currency stakeholders to establish their R&D Centers, innovation labs in Punjab, Pakistan
